Our Objectives

We are committed to a comprehensive approach to environmental welfare and sustainability.

1To help achieve an effective check on Environmental Pollution: Air, Water, Noise, Soil, Thermal, and Radiation Pollution, including control, prevention, protection, and promotion of sustainable development.
2To promote environmental education, raise awareness on environmental issues, and push for changes in the desired direction; to make public and government aware of environment-related health hazards.
3To contribute to development and implementation of environmental policy, planning, and to draw the attention of the international community and governments to pressing issues so that action can be taken on them.
4To promote environmental laws and create awareness thereof through conferences, rallies, programs, competitions, communication, and public information.
5To bring about comprehensive changes in people's thinking, economic and social structure, and help in streamlining the natural production and natural consumption.
6To contribute in conservation of natural biology, natural energy, renewable resources, and help in promoting environmental renewal.
7To contribute efforts in reducing greenhouse gas emissions worldwide and to promote and press for solutions for effective management of all kinds of waste.
8To help prevent/ combat environmental degradation and depletion of natural resources and nurture the Earth's ecological and cultural diversity and to safeguard sustainable livelihoods.
9To contribute in the making of all policies, strategies, and activities to protect water/natural resources of water and meet present and future human demand and thus avoid water scarcity.
10To help protect endangered species, wildlife and wildlife conservation, and to help in afforestation and reforestation initiatives, ensuring food security and good nutrition for one and all.
